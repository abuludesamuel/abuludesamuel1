THE BEGINNING

      My name is Abulude Samuel Opeyemi, I was born and brought up in a town called Osogbo in Osun State. I am a native of Otan Ayegbaju Boluwaduro local government in Osun state. I am a citizen of Nigeria. I am a yourba boy like I can speak the language fluently without and error as well as English which is the official language i can speak little of French language
   I was born into the family of Mr & Mrs Abulude in the early 2000s. It was an exciting day for my family becasue i am the first male child of the family which means i am the second born of the family, I have an elder sister, a younger sister and a your brother. We all live together to make a nuclear family. My father is presently a civil servant and my mother owns a Shop she is a trader.
   As a normal child who grows to crawl, walk , talk and grow a milk teeth i did all this to complete my early childhood.I play with different type of toys when i was young and my early childhood is an amazing one because i was pampered and showered with love.
    My first birthday was an amazing one we cut cake and everybody was happy been my first birthday my Dad bought me a gift of different types like clothes,football....etc.
    I started crawling from a spot to another for some months then I start to stand up with the help of a firm object like(Chair, table, stood) or holding someone to stand. This goes for some month as well, then i started walking but the walking is not quite smooth but rising and falling then the stuggle continues. When i couldn't walk well when my mates are already walking my Dad and Mum bought me a walker to enable me to walk, The tactics is really hard like whenever i hold the walker is having a three tyre in which when i push it it will move and i will walk with it so this enable me to walk well then my parents are happy.
    At the age of two I started talking but the speech is not really fluent then my parent concluded that i should be enrolled in a school
